Chapter 1: The Creek

Author's Note:

Honestly I'm not too sure why I abandoned this comic. I spent a solid 2 weeks staying up thinking about how I would structure each panel and pace the storyline, designing the villain etc. I guess I probably just got too lazy drawing all the backgrounds and stuff so I just moved on to doing other things. Pro tip its probably worth planning out small scenes that you'll use in the comic and sketching some out separate from the main story, just to keep yourself grounded and motivated to continue.
